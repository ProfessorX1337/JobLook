Your resume is the source. The AI is the editor.

What it reads, what it won't

The extension is narrow on purpose.

A tool that can fill a form can also exfiltrate one. We decided early that the simplest way to not abuse your trust is to not collect anything we don't need. Here's the exact line.

What JobLook does

  • Reads the labels, placeholders, and option lists on Greenhouse, Lever, and Ashby forms.
  • Reads the job description on the same page, so the AI draft is relevant.
  • Writes values from your profile into matching fields — nothing else.
  • Encrypts your resume at rest with per-row column keys (pgcrypto).

What it won't do

  • Submit any application on its own — the submit click is always yours.
  • Read or fill forms on any site outside the three ATS domains above.
  • Sell, share, or train third-party models on your resume or answers.
  • Keep a browsing history or collect analytics beyond install success.
