Our Story

JobLook was born from a simple frustration: spending hours filling out the same information across dozens of job applications. Our founders, experienced engineers who went through their own job searches, realized there had to be a better way.

After manually filling out over 200 applications between them, they decided to build the tool they wished existed. What started as a personal project has grown into a platform that helps thousands of professionals streamline their job search process.

We believe job searching should be about finding the right fit, not about copy-pasting the same information over and over. That's why we built JobLook to handle the repetitive tasks while preserving the personal touch that makes applications authentic.

Our Mission

To democratize access to career opportunities by removing barriers in the job application process. We believe everyone deserves a fair shot at their dream job, regardless of how much time they can spend on paperwork.

🔮

Our Vision

A world where job seekers can focus on showcasing their skills and finding cultural fit, while technology handles the repetitive administrative tasks that don't add value to the hiring process.

Our Values

We prioritize user privacy, data security, and transparency. We build tools that empower users without compromising their personal information or agency in the job search process.

How We Work

🔒 Privacy First

Your data belongs to you. We use minimal data collection, strong encryption, and give you complete control over your information.

🚀 Ship Fast, Iterate

We believe in rapid iteration based on user feedback. Our development cycle prioritizes getting useful features into users' hands quickly.

🤝 User-Centric

Every decision we make starts with asking: "How does this help job seekers?" We build based on real user needs, not assumptions.

📈 Transparent Growth

We share our journey, challenges, and wins openly. Our users deserve to know how we're building the company that serves them.

🎯 Quality Focus

We'd rather build one feature excellently than ten features poorly. Every release is tested thoroughly and refined based on feedback.

🌍 Remote First

Our distributed team spans multiple time zones and backgrounds, bringing diverse perspectives to solve complex problems.
